Engagement History

2014 - Higher Education - 2 months

Queen's University Faculty

Feasibility Analysis, Business Strategy

Key Challenges

The Client sought to determine the market size for an innovative online-only Bachelor's degree as a step in proving a business case to the Management.

solution development

Crafted a primary market research study and conducted secondary market research. Key data obtained was cross-referenced and used to analyze the post-secondary education market and identify the most attractive market segments.

End Result


Study revealed a viable market size and a follow up engagement was ordered by the Client to advise the program's marketing and financial strategy.

2014 - Public Administration - 3 months

Municipal Police Force

Business Planning, KPI Development, Marketing Strategy

Key Challenges

The Client sought to improve awareness and satisfaction ratings among its key stakeholders: the Community, the Employees, and Partner Organizations with limited human and financial resources.

solution development

Established direct relationship with the Chief of Police and Chairman of the Board to build a 3 year Ontario-regulated business plan. Created and executed primary market research study to analyze stakeholder profiles. Introduced KPI system to Management and facilitated goal setting exercises to produce S.M.A.R.T. KPIs.

End Result


A year following, the Force is now meeting or exceeding every KPI and is currently issuing follow-up market research to study stakeholder response.

2014 - Commercial Real Estate - 2 months

Property Manager

Exit Strategy, Commercial Real Estate Strategy

Key Challenges

The Client wished to gradually transition to a less physically intensive lifestyle and sought an exit for a $3M CAD portfolio in commercial real estate.

solution development

Began a profitability analysis of individual assets in portfolio followed by benchmarking portfolio against market comparables. Interviewed industry experts and curated an exit strategy based on the Client's lifestyle preferences and a unique market opportunity.

End Result


Comparable analysis indicated room for price raises leading to immediate realizations of profit opportunities. Market analysis led to a discovery of a high-return exit opportunity.

2014 - Health Care - 2 months

Private Practitioner

Growth Strategy, Feasibility Analysis, Marketing Strategy

Key Challenges

The Client sought to explore growth options for diversifying the revenue stream away from the main practice and into digital publishing media due to diminishing physical ability.

solution development

Following a revenue stream analysis of comparable industry practitioners, a gap analysis was performed to explore the Client's strength-to-market growth opportunities.

End Result


Feasibility and market analysis for existing digital products indicated new financially-sustainable markets for product line extensions.

2014 - Event Services - 3 months

Wedding Venue

Corporate Finance Strategy, Growth Strategy

Key Challenges

The returning Client sought a followup engagement, praising past strategy work performed by QBC. The Client sought to continue revenue growth and seek alternative sources of financing to avert a negative-equity business.

solution development

Established close working relationship with the Client's Chartered Accountant to build a DCF model and perform liquidity analysis. Followed up with research on alternative financing.

Conducted company and comparable analysis to find opportunities for revenue growth.

End Result


Financial research & analysis yielded feasible sources of fresh capital. Market research indicated room for general price increases to match comparable market standards.
