SparQ Labs is a space, a Makerspace, that provides tools like 3D Printers and Laser Cutters and the knowledge to build the next big thing.

SparQ Labs is the big idea, the idea that anyone can create the next big thing by having access to the right knowledge and resources.

SparQ Labs is the Maker Movement.

- The Story of -

SparQ Labs began in a spartan meeting room occupied beyond its humble capacity. Fast forward a few months and we were now sitting in a fully industrial facility sponsored by the University, fully dedicated to our operations.



Why did we start?



We started out with a burning desire. We were continuously bombarded with images of technological innovations all over the web, with the latest 3D printers and laser cutters all too fresh in our vivid memories. But what if we wanted to use one?

The only 3D printer on campus was the one locked behind administrative doors - requiring special permissions should you want to use one for restricted uses. So we developed an idea. Let us make a space with the latest tech available to every Queen's student and price it at cost.

And so with nothing more than a great desire and a foolhardy group of engineers and business students, SparQ Labs was born.
